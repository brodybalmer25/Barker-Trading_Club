About the Program

Where Futures Are Made

Founded in 2004, the Barker Trading Program at the University of Idaho transforms students into market-ready professionals through real-world experience in trading, risk management, and capital strategy.

Using institutional-grade platforms and live-market data, students develop and test trading strategies, manage funded accounts, and work in teams to run real portfolios. It's hands-on learning with real capital — and career-ready results.

Participants can earn a Trading and Capital Management Certificate, signaling to employers their comprehensive knowledge of financial markets, deep understanding of capital deployment, and strategic experience in managing risk.

Along the way, students connect with industry professionals, compete in events, and graduate ready to make an immediate impact in the finance world.
